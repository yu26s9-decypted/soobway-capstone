# Project Background

**Soobway** is a growing custom sandwich shop inspired by Subway.  
Until now, employees have taken orders off the top of their head and on paper, which has caused:

- slow checkout times
- incorrect orders
- pricing mistakes
- lost receipts
- customer frustration during busy lunch rush hours

The goal of the new Point of Sale system is to streamline the ordering process and improve order accuracy.

---

# User Story 1 — Jake (Customer)

## Character

Jake is a college student who visits Soobway during lunch breaks between classes.

## Goal

Jake wants to quickly customize a sandwich exactly how he likes it without repeating himself multiple times to employees.

## Concerns

- Orders being made incorrectly
- Long wait times
- Forgetting toppings
- Not knowing the total price before checkout

## User Story

> As a customer, Jake wants to customize his sandwich size, bread type, meats, cheeses, toppings, sauces, and drinks so that he can quickly place an accurate order and know the total cost before paying.

---

# User Story 2 — Chloe (Cashier)

## Character

Chloe is a cashier working during busy afternoon shifts.

## Goal

Chloe wants a simple interface that allows her to create customer orders quickly and clearly.

## Concerns

- Holding up the line
- Misclicking toppings
- Forgetting premium charges
- Handwriting mistakes from paper orders

## User Story

> As a cashier, Chloe wants the system to guide customers through the sandwich-building process step-by-step so that orders are entered consistently and accurately during busy hours.

---

# User Story 3 — Max (Store Manager)

## Character

Max is the manager of the Soobway location.

## Goal

Max wants every order saved digitally for tracking and accountability.

## Concerns

- Lost paper receipts
- Incorrect pricing
- Employees forgetting extra topping charges
- Difficulty reviewing past orders

## User Story

> As a store manager, Max wants all completed orders to automatically generate receipt files so that transactions can be tracked and reviewed later.

---

# User Story 4 — Sarah (Health-Conscious Customer)

## Character

Sarah prefers healthier meal options and frequently customizes her food.

## Goal

Sarah wants flexibility when building her sandwich.

## Concerns

- Limited customization
- Being charged incorrectly for regular toppings
- Not seeing all available options

## User Story

> As a customer, Sarah wants to freely choose from regular toppings, premium toppings, condiments, and specialty options so that she can fully customize her meal to fit her preferences.

---

# User Story 5 — Ethan (New Employee)

## Character

Ethan recently started working at Soobway.

## Goal

Ethan wants the system to be easy to learn without memorizing prices.

## Concerns

- Accidentally charging the wrong amount
- Forgetting extra meat pricing
- Getting overwhelmed during rush hours

## User Story

> As a new employee, Ethan wants the system to automatically calculate pricing for sandwich sizes and premium toppings so that I can process orders confidently without memorizing every price of menu items.
